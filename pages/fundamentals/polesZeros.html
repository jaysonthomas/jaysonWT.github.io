Musings

Assume that the position error trend can be approximated by a second order system with two complex conjugate poles (to model the oscillations) and a zero (at low frequency to model the overshoot), the mean of the error mean(y) could move away from the steady value r . So I was thinking, instead of using the average, that I could fit the y(t) expression to the data in order to identify the parameters, in particular r (keeping the assumption that the error can be actually modelled as a second order system).
