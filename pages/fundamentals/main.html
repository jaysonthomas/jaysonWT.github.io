    Probability density

    Ref1, Ref2. It's not a probability. It's a probability over a given area/volume. It's used when the distribution is continuous. If it was discrete and we wanted to know the probability of 1 single event, it would be called a probability mass. The function that gives the probability density over a given area is called the probability density function.
