Overview

Let's apply the formulation to a 2R robot in gravity. All the mass of the robot is concentrated in point masses $m_1$ and $m_2$. We need to calculate the kinetic and potential energy of the two-point masses, so first we calculate the position of $m_1$, given by the coordinates $x_1$ and $y_1$.
