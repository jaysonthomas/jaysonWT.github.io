LTI: Setup

The collection of linear operations mentioned here, are represented by the linear operator $h$: $$ y(t) = \underbrace{h(x(t))}_{\text{Linear} \atop {\text{system}}} $$ $h$ provides a linear mapping between the vector $x(t)$ and the vector $y(t)$
