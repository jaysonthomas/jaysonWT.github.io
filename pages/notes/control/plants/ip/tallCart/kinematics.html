Overview

When the cart rotates by a small angle $\theta$ about its base, the top of the cart moves along an arc of a circle with radius $l$ and displacement (relative to the vertical line through the base) given by the horizontal component of the arc. When $\theta$ is small, the small angle approximation can be used: $\sin\theta \approx \theta$ $$ \Delta{x}_{top} = l \, sin\theta $$
