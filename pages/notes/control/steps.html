Control: Steps

Solving a control problem also involves solving the following problems:

System thinking

  • Inputs-Outputs: Forces acting on an actuator and on a system via the actuator; what is the end result
  • What is being measured (the end result above), errors, timing delays

System identification

  • What parts of a system to represent
  • What representation to use
    • Transfer functions vs State-Space vs Differential equations
    • LTI systems or not

Simulation
